<p><p>Industrial stretch wrap films are categorized into two main types: machine stretch film and hand stretch wrap. Machine stretch film is designed for automated packaging operations, typically used with stretch wrap machines that efficiently wrap pallets and products for secure shipping. It offers consistent tension and high production speeds. In contrast, hand stretch wrap is used manually, providing flexibility for smaller operations or irregularly shaped loads. It is ideal for less frequent wrapping where precision and portability are essential.</p></p>
