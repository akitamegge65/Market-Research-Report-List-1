<p><strong>The Laboratory Safety Cabinets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class I Biological Safety Cabinets</li><li>Class II Biological Safety Cabinets</li><li>Class III Biological Safety Cabinet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory safety cabinets are essential for protecting users and the environment from hazardous materials. Class I Biological Safety Cabinets provide personnel protection but do not offer product protection from contamination. Class II Biological Safety Cabinets combine personnel, product, and environmental protection, featuring a laminar airflow system, making them ideal for handling infectious agents. Class III Biological Safety Cabinets provide the highest level of protection, fully enclosed and isolated, allowing manipulation of highly infectious pathogens while ensuring maximum safety for users and the surrounding environment.</p></p>
