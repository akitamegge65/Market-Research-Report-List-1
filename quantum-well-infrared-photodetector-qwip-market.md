<p><p>Quantum Well Infrared Photodetectors (QWIPs) are semiconductor devices that utilize quantum wells to detect infrared light, benefiting from enhanced sensitivity and tunability. The market comprises two primary types: Light Guide Type QWIPs, which utilize optical fibers or guides to funnel infrared light into the detector, enhancing efficiency and performance; and Photovoltaic Type QWIPs, which generate a photocurrent upon absorbing infrared photons, directly converting light into electrical signals. Both types are essential for applications in security, surveillance, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
