<p><p>The WiFi security camera market is divided primarily into two types: local storage and cloud storage. Local storage type cameras save recorded footage on physical devices like SD cards or hard drives, allowing users complete control over their data without recurring fees. Conversely, cloud storage type cameras store footage on remote servers accessed via the internet, offering convenience and off-site backups but often requiring monthly subscriptions. Both types cater to varying user preferences regarding data management, security, and accessibility.</p></p>
