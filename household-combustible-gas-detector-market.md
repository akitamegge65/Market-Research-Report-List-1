<p><ul><li>Catalytic Combustion Sensor</li><li>Semiconductor Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Household Combustible Gas Detector market primarily features two types of sensors: catalytic combustion sensors and semiconductor sensors. Catalytic combustion sensors detect gases by oxidizing them over a catalyst, resulting in a temperature increase that indicates gas presence. They are highly sensitive and suitable for a variety of combustible gases. Conversely, semiconductor sensors work by measuring changes in electrical conductivity when gases are absorbed, making them cost-effective and compact. Both types are essential for enhancing safety in residential environments.</p></p>
