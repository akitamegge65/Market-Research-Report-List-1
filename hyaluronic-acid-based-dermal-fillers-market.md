<p><p>The hyaluronic acid-based dermal fillers market is characterized by two main types: single-phase products and duplex products. Single-phase products consist of a uniform gel texture, providing smooth and even volume to the skin. They are typically easier to inject and offer natural results. Duplex products, on the other hand, feature a two-layer structure, combining gels of varying densities to address different aesthetic needs, enabling both volume enhancement and improved skin texture for a more sophisticated treatment approach.</p></p>
