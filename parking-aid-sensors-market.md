<p><p>Parking aid sensors enhance vehicle safety and convenience by assisting drivers during parking maneuvers. The market is primarily segmented into front parking sensors and rear parking sensors. Front parking sensors detect obstacles in the vehicle's path while maneuvering forward, alerting the driver to potential hazards. Rear parking sensors, on the other hand, provide assistance when reversing, helping to prevent collisions with vehicles or objects behind the car. Both types of sensors are crucial for improving parking accuracy and reducing accident risks.</p></p>
