<p><strong>The Vinyl Flooring Tiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Vinyl Tile</li><li>Rigid Vinyl Tile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vinyl flooring tiles market primarily comprises two types: flexible vinyl tiles (FVT) and rigid vinyl tiles (RVT). Flexible vinyl tiles are soft and adaptable, making them easy to install and suitable for a variety of surfaces, offering comfort and sound absorption. In contrast, rigid vinyl tiles feature a sturdier core that provides enhanced durability and resilience, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Both types offer a versatile range of designs and are popular for their affordability and maintenance ease.</p></p>
