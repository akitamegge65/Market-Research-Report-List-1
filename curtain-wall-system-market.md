<p><p>The curtain wall system market comprises three main types: Component Curtain Walls, Pressed Component Curtain Walls, and Hinged Component Curtain Walls. Component Curtain Walls are prefabricated, allowing for modular assembly on-site, enhancing design flexibility. Pressed Component Curtain Walls utilize stamped or pressed materials, providing a streamlined manufacturing process and cost-effectiveness. Hinged Component Curtain Walls feature movable elements that facilitate ventilation and access, combining aesthetics with functionality. Collectively, these systems cater to diverse architectural needs and preferences in modern construction.</p></p>
