<p><p>The solvent cements and primers market consists of various types catering to different types of plastic materials. PVC solvent cement is used for bonding polyvinyl chloride pipes and fittings, ensuring strong joints. CPVC solvent cement is designed for chlorinated polyvinyl chloride applications, suitable for hot and cold water systems. ABS solvent cement is formulated for acrylonitrile-butadiene-styrene products, providing robust connections. Primers are used to prepare surfaces before applying solvent cements, enhancing adhesion and ensuring long-lasting bonds across these materials.</p></p>
