<p><p>The foreline traps market encompasses three primary types: particle traps, liquid traps, and gas traps. Particle traps are designed to capture solid contaminants from gas streams, enhancing purity. Liquid traps prevent the ingress of liquids into vacuum systems, protecting sensitive equipment. Gas traps, on the other hand, capture specific gaseous contaminants, ensuring optimal performance of vacuum pumps. Together, these traps are essential for maintaining vacuum system integrity across various applications, including semiconductor manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and research laboratories.</p></p>
