<p><p>Textile lamination adhesives are categorized into several types based on their composition and application methods. Water-based lamination adhesives utilize water as a solvent, offering a safer and eco-friendly option. Solvent-based laminating adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding but may involve health concerns. Solvent-free laminating adhesives eliminate solvents entirely, promoting sustainability. Dry laminating adhesives are applied in a solid form, requiring heat for activation. Other types may include specialty adhesives designed for specific textile laminating needs and applications.</p></p>
