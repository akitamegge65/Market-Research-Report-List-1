<p><strong>The Implantable Venous Port Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Lumen</li><li>Double-Lumen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implantable venous port market is segmented into single-lumen and double-lumen ports. Single-lumen ports feature one access point, making them ideal for patients requiring straightforward intravenous therapy or medication administration. In contrast, double-lumen ports have two separate channels, allowing simultaneous access for multiple treatments, blood sampling, or intravenous infusions. This versatility in double-lumen systems caters to patients with complex medical needs, enhancing treatment efficiency and reducing the frequency of port access. Both types are critical in oncology and chronic disease management.</p></p>
