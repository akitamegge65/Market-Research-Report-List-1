<p><strong>The Fiber Drop Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Cable</li><li>Multi Mode Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber drop cable market comprises two primary types: single mode cable and multi-mode cable. Single mode cables utilize a single light path, enabling long-distance data transmission with minimal loss, making them ideal for telecommunication and long-haul networks. In contrast, multi-mode cables allow multiple light paths, which is effective for shorter distances, such as within buildings or campuses. Both types cater to specific applications, differentiating by performance, cost, and installation environments, thus serving diverse connectivity needs.</p></p>
