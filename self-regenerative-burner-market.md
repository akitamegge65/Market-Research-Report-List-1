<p><p>The self-regenerative burner market is divided into two main types: rotary burners and non-rotating burners. Rotary burners utilize a rotating mechanism that continuously cycles heat between combustion and heat recovery, improving efficiency and reducing emissions. In contrast, non-rotating burners employ a static design, focusing on effective heat exchange without movement. Both types aim to enhance thermal efficiency and minimize energy consumption, making them suitable for various industrial applications in sectors like manufacturing and energy generation.</p></p>
