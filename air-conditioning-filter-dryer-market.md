<p><strong>The Air Conditioning Filter Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Line Type</li><li>Suction Line Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning filter dryer market is categorized into two main types: Liquid Line Type and Suction Line Type. The Liquid Line Type is installed in the refrigerant liquid line, filtering out moisture and impurities before they reach the expansion device, ensuring optimal system performance. Conversely, the Suction Line Type is placed in the suction line, targeting moisture prevention and compressor protection during low-pressure conditions. Both types are essential for enhancing the efficiency and longevity of HVAC systems.</p></p>
