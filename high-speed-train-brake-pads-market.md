<p><p>The high-speed train brake pads market comprises three main types: iron-based, copper-based, and composite brake pads. Iron-based brake pads offer durability and thermal stability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Copper-based brake pads provide excellent friction characteristics but are facing environmental scrutiny due to copper emissions. Composite brake pads, made from a blend of materials, offer a balance of performance, reduced weight, and lower noise levels. Each type has its advantages, catering to different operational needs and performance standards in high-speed rail systems.</p></p>
