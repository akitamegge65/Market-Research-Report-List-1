<p><p>Single Inlet Centrifugal Brushless DC Fans are categorized into three main types based on their impeller designs: forward-curved, backward-curved, and straight radial. Forward-curved fans are known for high airflow at low resistance, making them ideal for ventilation applications. Backward-curved fans offer higher efficiency and pressure capabilities, suitable for demanding industrial settings. Straight radial fans provide balanced airflow and pressure characteristics, often used in applications requiring moderate airflow and efficient operation. Each type caters to specific performance needs in various markets.</p></p>
