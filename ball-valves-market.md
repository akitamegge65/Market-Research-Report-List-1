<p><strong>The Ball Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trunnion Mounted</li><li>Floating</li><li>Rising Stem</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball valves are categorized into three main types: trunnion mounted, floating, and rising stem. Trunnion mounted valves have a support structure that minimizes friction, making them suitable for high-pressure applications, while floating ball valves have a ball that floats, allowing better sealing under low-pressure conditions. Rising stem ball valves feature a stem that rises as the valve opens, providing clear visual confirmation of valve position. Each type is designed to enhance performance for specific operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
