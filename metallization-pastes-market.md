<p><p>The metallization pastes market is segmented into three main types based on their application in solar cells. Front-side silver (Ag) paste is crucial for creating conductive pathways on the surface of solar cells, enhancing efficiency. Rear-side silver paste serves a similar purpose but is applied to the back, optimizing overall performance. Rear-side aluminum (Al) paste is utilized for forming the back electrode, providing essential reflectivity and electrical contact. Each type plays a vital role in improving solar cell efficiency and performance.</p></p>
