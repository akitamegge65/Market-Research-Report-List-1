<p><strong>The Native Salt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sea Salt</li><li>Lake Salt</li><li>Well and Rock Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Native salt markets are categorized into four primary types: sea salt, lake salt, well salt, and rock salt. Sea salt is harvested by evaporating seawater, providing natural minerals. Lake salt is extracted from saltwater lakes, often rich in various elements. Well salt comes from underground brine wells, where saltwater is pumped and evaporated. Rock salt is mined from underground salt deposits, formed over centuries, and is typically less processed. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and culinary uses.</p></p>
