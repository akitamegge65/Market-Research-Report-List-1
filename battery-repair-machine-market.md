<p><p>The battery repair machine market is segmented into three main types: Pulse Repair, Step Wave Repair, and Plasma Repair. Pulse Repair utilizes short electrical pulses to rejuvenate battery cells, enhancing their performance. Step Wave Repair employs a series of rising and falling voltage levels, effectively balancing cell discharge rates. Plasma Repair uses plasma technology to clean and repair battery components, improving overall efficiency. Each method targets specific battery issues, offering innovative solutions for extending battery life and performance.</p></p>
