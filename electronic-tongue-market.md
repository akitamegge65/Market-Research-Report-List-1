<p><strong>The Electronic Tongue Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometric Type</li><li>Amperometric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic tongue market comprises various types, notably potentiometric and amperometric types. The potentiometric type measures ion concentrations through voltage changes, providing insights into the chemical composition of liquids. In contrast, the amperometric type detects current changes resulting from electrochemical reactions, enabling rapid analysis of substances like glucose or heavy metals. Both types are extensively used in food, beverage, and environmental testing, offering manufacturers efficient tools for quality control and compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
