<p><strong>The Tumor Cryoablation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phase Change Refrigeration</li><li>Joule-Thomson Throttling Refrigeration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tumor Cryoablation Device Market includes two primary refrigeration types: Phase Change Refrigeration and Joule-Thomson Throttling Refrigeration. Phase Change Refrigeration utilizes a refrigerant that changes its state from liquid to gas, absorbing heat and achieving low temperatures to destroy tumor cells. In contrast, Joule-Thomson Throttling Refrigeration relies on the expansion of gas to create cooling effects, effectively freezing and eliminating targeted tissues. Both methods are effective in non-surgical tumor treatment and enhance patient care in oncology.</p></p>
