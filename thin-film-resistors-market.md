<p><p>Thin-film resistors are precision components used in various electronic applications, known for their high accuracy, low noise, and temperature stability. These resistors are commonly used in industries such as telecommunications, automotive, medical devices, and consumer electronics, where high precision and reliability are paramount.</p><p>The Thin-Film Resistors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period.
