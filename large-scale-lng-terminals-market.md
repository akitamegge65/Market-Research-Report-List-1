<p><strong>The Large-scale LNG Terminals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquefaction</li><li>Regasification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large-scale LNG terminals market is primarily divided into two types: liquefaction and regasification. Liquefaction terminals convert natural gas into liquefied natural gas (LNG) for efficient storage and transport, mainly located near gas supply sources. Conversely, regasification terminals receive LNG shipments and convert them back into gas for distribution into pipelines, typically situated near consumption areas. Both types play crucial roles in the global LNG supply chain, facilitating trade and enhancing energy security.</p></p>
