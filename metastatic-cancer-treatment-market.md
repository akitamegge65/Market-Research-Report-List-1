<p><p>The metastatic cancer treatment market consists of various approaches targeting advanced cancer. Chemotherapy uses cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing cancer cells. Radiation therapy employs high-energy radiation to shrink tumors and alleviate symptoms. Biologic targeted therapy focuses on specific molecular targets within cancer cells, minimizing damage to normal cells. Hormone therapy interferes with hormone production, essential for certain cancers' growth. Together, these treatments aim to manage symptoms, prolong survival, and improve the quality of life for patients with metastatic cancer.</p></p>
